About Me

Rooted in Physics. Driven by Data. Dedicated to National Strategy.

I serve as an Assistant Director at the President's Office of Sri Lanka, representing the Sri Lanka Planning Service. In this capacity, I focus on National Development Planning, bridging the gap between country-level strategy and data-driven decision-making by applying scientific rigor to public administration.

My academic and professional roots are deeply embedded in the physical sciences. I am a Licentiate Member of the Institute of Physics, Sri Lanka, and previously worked as a Metrologist specializing in ISO/IEC 17025 standards. This dedication to precision is reflected in my academic career, where I graduated Top of the Batch in my M.Sc. in Applied Statistics

With a strong mathematical foundation, I have a deep passion for working with Machine Learning algorithms and exploring their theoretical underpinnings. Beyond my official duties, I remain active in Statistics and Data Science with a portfolio of over 600 projects. I also actively engage in Full Stack Development to refine my engineering skills, with a clear vision to evolve into AI Engineering solving complex problems at the intersection of Math, Code, and Strategy.
